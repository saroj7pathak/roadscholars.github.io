U.S. Roads: A look at our roads

The purpose of this project is to analyze and visualize pavement data made available by the Department of Transportation (DoT). See our About page for details on the program and the data.






- 87 percent of daily trips take place in personal vehicles

- 91 percent of people commuting to work use personal vehicles









- 45 percent of daily trips are taken for shopping and errands

- 27 percent of daily trips are social and recreational, such as visiting a friend

- 15 percent of daily trips are taken for commuting



On average, women drive less and men drive longer.

The average driver spends 55 minutes a day behind the wheel
